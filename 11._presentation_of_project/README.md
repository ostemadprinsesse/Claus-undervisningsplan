## Before Class
You should in your group have cretaed a presentation of your project.    
Do not use slides! but instead focus on showing what you did so far.     
It is expected that your project have a functioning CD/CD pipeline setup in place, which includes the topics we covered so far.     
It is also important that everybody in your group takes ownership of your project. That does not mean that everybody is an expert in all areas of the setup, but it means that you have some ereas where you are the expert.
